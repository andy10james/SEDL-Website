About SEDL Europe

Welcome to SEDL Europe, founded January 1st 2011, we specialise in online gaming communities for XBOX Live catering for your personality and style of play. We provide events around our dedicated members for games including Call of Duty, Halo 3, Halo: Reach, Crysis 2 and many other games depending on the community you choose with SEDL. We are in 4 groups; Project Solar, Project Eclipse, Project Destiny and Project Legacy.

Project Solar is an open join group in friendly league with Project Eclipse, and is one of our primary groups as a leading contender against other groups across Europe. Members are involved in activities and events with appreciation from SEDL, and eventually, those who prove worthy could join Destiny.

Project Eclipse is also an open join group in friendly league with Project Solar, and is one of our primary groups as a leading contender against other groups across Europe. Members are involved in activities and events with appreciation from SEDL, and eventually, those who prove worthy could join Destiny.

Destiny is where everyone wants to be, its the group that represents the most worthy of SEDL and those who will be treated with the most respect on XBOX Live as a result of their ongoing effort and support to the SEDL Community. SEDL Destiny members recieve exclusive access to all areas and have large say SEDL and where it goes.

Legacy is an exclusive group reserved for only the dedicated of members from the original ProLegends Network. Members will receive individual appreciation from SEDL and be included in exclusive events. SEDL Legacy represents and preserves where SEDL began.
