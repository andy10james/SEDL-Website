The SEDL Forum is our online community home, you will make new friends with in Solar, Eclipse, Destiny and Legacy and discuss the hot topics about XBOX Live and SEDL within your Group and with other group members in the game forums.

New to Forums? Haven't registered yet?

Only SEDL Members can usally post on these forums, so if you are an SEDL Member, register for an account on the SEDL Europe Forum, remember this is a different system to SEDL Oracle, new username, new password. After registration, you have read access to all posts (Except for Destiny ofcourse). To start posting as an SEDL Member, head on over to the User Control Panel, Select Usergroups, and apply for your division. If you belong to Legacy or Destiny, your membership will be pending division director approval. Once approved others can see you online as a member of one of SEDL branch groups and you will be able to post in your groups forum and other open forums.

Have fun and Contact support@sedl.eu for any queries or issues with Forum Network.
